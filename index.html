About Me

My name is Jay Baath. I am originally from St. Louis, MO but have lived in Houston most of my life. I attended the University of Texas at Austin until 2016. Currently, I work with my father in the field of real estate and property management involving various small businesses and properties.

In my spare time I enjoy watching movies, working out, and playing sports. I have a 12 year old chocolate Labrador Retriever named Brownie with whom I enjoy playing outdoors with and taking on walks.
